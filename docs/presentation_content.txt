SLIDE 4 — HOW IT WORKS
-----------------------
Headline: Upload a BOM. Get a risk report in 90 seconds.

[Box 1] SUPPLY RISK
Computes HHI concentration score and FEOC compliance flag per material
Source: USGS Mineral Commodity Summaries 2025 + OEC Trade Data 2023

[Box 2] FAILURE MODES
Surfaces real NHTSA vehicle recalls classified by failure mode and severity
Source: 8 EV recalls, 141,667+ vehicles affected, 2018–2026

[Box 3] SUBSTITUTIONS
3 ranked alternative materials with full property delta comparison
Source: Materials Project API (Lawrence Berkeley National Lab)

[Box 4] QUALIFICATION ROADMAP
Testing standards, timeline in weeks, and cost band in USD
Standards: UN 38.3 · IEC 62660-1/2 · UL 2580 · ISO 26262 · ECE R100

SLIDE 7 — HALLUCINATION GUARD
------------------------------
Headline: Every number is cited. By design.

WITHOUT MATRES              |  WITH MATRES
"Cobalt is risky"           |  "Cobalt HHI: 5,535 · Source: USGS MCS 2025"
"LFP is safer"              |  "LFP supply risk: 10/100 · Source: NREL 2023"
"Qualification takes months"|  "80 weeks · $925k–$1.85M · Source: UN 38.3, IEC 62660"
"China dominates graphite"  |  "China: 77% share · HHI 6,058 · Source: USGS MCS 2025"

A CitationError middleware rejects any numeric output that lacks a source field
before it reaches the UI. No uncited numbers can appear in the report.
